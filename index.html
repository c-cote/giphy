See those buttons of Inuyasha characters? As if that list wasn't enough, you can use the textbox to add your own. Just type in the name of one of the characters, like Shippo or Ryn, and click the Add a Character button. A button with your character's name will be added to the list. All you need to do then is click on the button and (hopefully) all kinds of sweet .gifs will appear below.

After the .gifs are loaded, you may click on one to view a still image instead of the animated version. Click on it again to restore the image to its frenetic state. Enjoy!
